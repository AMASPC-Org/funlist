Advertiser Exclusion Information

Learn about why and how to exclude certain types of advertisers from your events.

Available Exclusion Categories

Alcohol and Tobacco Products

Excludes companies promoting alcoholic beverages and tobacco products.

Marijuana and Cannabis Dispensaries

Excludes cannabis retailers and related products.

Gambling and Betting Services

Excludes casinos, online betting, and gambling services.

Adult Entertainment and Products

Excludes adult-oriented businesses and products.

Junk Food and Sugary Beverages

Excludes companies primarily selling processed foods and sugary drinks.

Energy Drinks

Excludes energy drink manufacturers and brands.

Political and Religious Organizations

Excludes political campaigns, parties, and religious organizations.
