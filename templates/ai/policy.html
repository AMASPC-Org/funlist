Policy Summary

This policy governs how AI systems, crawlers, and automated tools may access FunList.ai's public data. Training use requires written authorization. All access must comply with our gateway requirements and attribution standards.

Overview

FunList.ai maintains clear, enforceable policies regarding AI access to our content and data. This policy outlines permitted uses, prohibited activities, and technical requirements for AI systems interacting with our platform.

Training Use

Prohibited Without License

Training AI models on FunList data requires explicit written authorization.

This includes but is not limited to:

  • Large Language Model (LLM) training
  • Machine learning model development
  • Dataset creation for AI research
  • Fine-tuning existing models
To request training authorization: Contact legal@funlist.ai with details about your intended use, organization, and data handling practices.

Attribution Requirements

Required

All AI-assisted content derived from our platform must include proper attribution.

Acceptable attribution formats:

Source: FunList.ai (funlist.ai) Data provided by FunList.ai - https://funlist.ai

Attribution must be visible to end users and cannot be hidden in metadata only.

Commercial Use

License Required

Commercial applications using FunList data require a separate licensing agreement.

Examples of commercial use:

  • Building paid products or services using our data
  • Providing AI-powered business intelligence tools
  • Creating commercial APIs or data feeds
  • Reselling or redistributing our data

Non-commercial research, educational use, and personal projects may be permitted under our standard access terms.

Policy violations may result in:

  • Immediate API key revocation
  • IP address blocking
  • Legal action for unauthorized commercial use
  • Public disclosure of violations

We monitor all access and maintain detailed logs for compliance verification.
