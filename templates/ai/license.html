License Terms

This license governs the use of data and content from FunList.ai by artificial intelligence systems, automated crawlers, and other programmatic access methods.

Effective Date: November 2025 | Version: 1.0

Permitted Uses

Allowed
  • Search & Indexing: Accessing public event data through our API endpoints for search purposes
  • Citation & Reference: Using our data with proper attribution to FunList
  • Analysis: Non-commercial research and analysis with appropriate disclosure
  • Integration: Displaying our events in applications with proper attribution

Prohibited Uses

Not Allowed
  • AI Model Training: Using our data to train LLMs or other AI models without explicit written license
  • Commercial Resale: Redistributing or reselling our data without licensing agreement
  • Unauthorized Access: Circumventing authentication, rate limits, or access controls
  • Missing Attribution: Using our data without proper source attribution
  • Scraping: Unauthorized automated scraping outside our official API
Violations will result in: Immediate API key revocation, IP blocking, and potential legal action.

License Application Process

To apply for a license for AI training, commercial use, or other special purposes, submit a request to legal@funlist.ai including:

  • Organization Details: Name, website, and primary contact information
  • Intended Use Case: Detailed description of how you plan to use our data
  • Expected Volume: Estimated requests per day/month
  • Attribution Method: How you will credit FunList in your application
  • Data Handling: How you will store, process, and protect our data

We typically respond to license requests within 5 business days.
