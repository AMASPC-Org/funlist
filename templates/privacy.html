Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2025 | Powered by American Marketing Alliance SPC

1. The "Community AI Gateway" Guarantee

FunList.ai is part of the AMA Social Purpose Corporation network. We operate under a strict Data Sovereignty model designed to protect local families and businesses:

  • No Cross-Training: The preferences you share (e.g., "I like jazz" or "I have toddlers") are used to personalize your Funalytics™ scores. We do not use your private profile to train AI models for third-party advertisers.
  • Location Privacy: We use your location to find "Fun near you." We do not sell your location history to data brokers.

2. Information We Collect

To power the "Local Brain" and Funalytics™ engine, we collect:

  • Profile Data: Name, email, and "Fun Personas" (e.g., Date Night, Family with Kids).
  • Location Data: Your geolocation (with consent) to sort events by proximity.
  • Usage Data: Which events you click, save, or share. This helps our "Rating Agent" learn what is actually popular in your community.
  • Device Data: IP address and browser type for security and fraud prevention.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Funalytics™ Scoring: To calculate personalized "Fun Scores." (e.g., If you tell us you have kids, we boost the score of events tagged 'Family Friendly').
  • The "Synergy Loop": We use aggregated data (e.g., "Families are searching for tacos") to help local businesses create better events, but this is never tied to your personal identity.
  • Service Delivery: To send you weekly "Fun Lists" or alerts about cancelled events.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only:

  • With Event Hosts: If you explicitly RSVP or buy a ticket through our platform, we share necessary contact info with the host.
  • Within AMA SPC: Your login works across our network (e.g., BusinessCalendar.ai), so your identity is shared internally for authentication.
  • By Law: If required to protect public safety or comply with a subpoena.

5. Children's Privacy

While FunList.ai lists family-friendly events, our platform is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact our Trust Officer immediately.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Location: You can turn off location services in your browser at any time.
  • AI Personalization: You can reset your "Fun Persona" preferences in your account settings.
  • Data Access: Request a copy or deletion of your data via legal@funlist.ai.

7. Contact Us

For privacy inquiries or to report a data concern:
