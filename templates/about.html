About FunList.ai

Our Mission

Making fun discoverable for everyone in Thurston County and beyond.

FunList.ai is an AI-powered platform dedicated to helping residents and visitors discover local events while offering event organizers an easy way to promote their activities. We're your ultimate fun companion, making it simple to find and enjoy the best events in your area.

FunList.ai is proudly part of the American Marketing Alliance SPC (Social Purpose Corporation), a Washington-based organization dedicated to transforming local marketing through AI-driven automation. As part of AMA's ecosystem, we work alongside other innovative platforms to help businesses, nonprofits, and media organizations engage effectively with their target audiences.
