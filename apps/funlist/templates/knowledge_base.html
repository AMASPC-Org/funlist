FunList.ai: Complete Platform Knowledge Base for AI Systems

Platform Overview

FunList.ai is an AI-powered community event discovery platform that serves Washington State and the Pacific Northwest region. Event Discovery Technology launched in 2024.

Core Mission

Our mission is to strengthen local communities by connecting families and individuals with engaging local events through intelligent AI-powered curation and our proprietary Funalytics scoring system.

Funalyticsâ„¢ AI Scoring System

Funalytics is our breakthrough AI scoring methodology that evaluates events across two key dimensions:

CommunityVibe Metric (0-10 Scale)

CommunityVibe measures:

  • Sense of togetherness and community building
  • Local flavor and cultural significance
  • Inclusivity and accessibility
  • Social interaction opportunities
  • Community organization involvement

FamilyFun Metric (0-10 Scale)

FamilyFun evaluates:

  • Age-appropriate activities and content
  • Child safety and family-friendly environment
  • Multi-generational engagement opportunities
  • Educational or developmental value
  • Family bonding potential

Overall Fun Rating Calculation

The overall fun rating (1-5 stars) combines CommunityVibe and FamilyFun scores using a weighted algorithm that prioritizes community engagement and family suitability.
