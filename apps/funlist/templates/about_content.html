About FunList.ai: AI-Powered Community Event Discovery

Connecting communities through intelligent event discovery and our proprietary Funalytics™ scoring system.

What is FunList.ai?

FunList.ai is the leading AI-powered platform for discovering community events in Washington State and beyond. Our mission is to help families, individuals, and community organizations connect through shared experiences and local activities.

Key Features

  • Funalytics™ Scoring: Our proprietary AI system rates events on CommunityVibe™ and FamilyFun™ metrics
  • Interactive Event Map: Discover events by location with real-time filtering
  • AI Fun Assistant: Get personalized event recommendations
  • Community-Focused: Events curated for local engagement and family participation

The Funalytics™ System Explained

Funalytics™ is our breakthrough scoring methodology that evaluates events across two key dimensions:

CommunityVibe™ (0-10 Scale)

Measures sense of togetherness, local flavor, and inclusivity. Events with strong community connection score higher.

FamilyFun™ (0-10 Scale)

Evaluates suitability for families and children, considering age-appropriate activities and family engagement opportunities.
